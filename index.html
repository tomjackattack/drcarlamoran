About

Dr Carla Moran is a consultant endocrinologist based in Dublin with specialist expertise in thyroid disease, rare endocrine disorders and endocrine disease in pregnancy. She completed specialist training in Endocrinology, Diabetes Mellitus and General Internal Medicine in Ireland before undertaking a fellowship in thyroid disease at Addenbrooke’s Hospital and the University of Cambridge, where she obtained her PhD.
